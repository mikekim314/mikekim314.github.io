Our History


First Fruits Farm was founded with a simple vision: to give back the "first fruits" of our labor to those in need. What began as a small family project has blossomed into a community-wide ministry.

Every year, thousands of volunteers gather to plant, harvest, and distribute millions of pounds of fresh potatoes, corn, green beans, and apples to food banks and shelters across the Mid-Atlantic region.

We believe that love is an action, and we invite you to join us in the fields.
